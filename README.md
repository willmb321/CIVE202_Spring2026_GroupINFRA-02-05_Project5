# CIVE202_Spring2026_GroupINFRA-02-05_Project5

The goal of this project is to analyze natural hazards in the United States, specifically six states that are located close to one another. Analyzing six states located by each other will allow our team to make regional conclusions. Additionally, LLC wants our team to choose a specific hazard and find information regarding the population impacted by that natural hazard. LLC has requested two key analyses: (1) a summary plot showing how the natural disaster differs across the six different states, and (2) an analysis that shows how this natural disaster risk relates to the population of each state.
